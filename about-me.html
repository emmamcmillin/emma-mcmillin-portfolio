About Me

Hi, I'm Emma McMillin, a passionate web developer and designer in my final year at SAIT. With a keen eye for detail and a love for clean, user-friendly design, I specialize in crafting seamless digital experiences that are both functional and visually engaging.

My Journey

My journey into web development started with a curiosity about how websites come to life. Over time, I honed my skills in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP, and MySQL, allowing me to build dynamic, responsive, and accessible websites. My experience working on projects like a timesheet application for freelance developers and a fitness website with its own shop has strengthened my ability to solve real-world problems through code.

What I Bring to the Table

  • Back-End Development Building secure, efficient database-driven applications.
  • User Experience (UX) & Accessibility Prioritizing usability and inclusivity in every design.
  • Problem-Solving & Collaboration Adapting quickly to challenges and working effectively in teams.
